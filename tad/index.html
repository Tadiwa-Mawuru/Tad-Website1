Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences

The Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences produces well skilled, highly qualified professional medical doctors
and practitioners to meet healthcare needs of the people of Zimbabwe. It houses twelve teaching departments that offer
a wide variety of educational, research and career options within the context of health care. It take hard work and
dedication to become a competent pharmacist , physiotherapist ,occupational therapist ,nurse ,dentist ,medical laboratory
scientist ,radiographer ,or doctor ,which is exactly what students are taught. The Faculty takes academic excellence as
a characteristic of its existence by promoting science through research.
